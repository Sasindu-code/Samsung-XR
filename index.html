What Samsung XR Is

Samsung XR is Samsung’s extended-reality platform centered on the Galaxy XR headset,
a standalone device that combines virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and mixed
reality into one experience. Built in partnership with Google and Qualcomm, it runs Android
XR and uses advanced sensors, cameras, and AI to let users interact naturally through
voice, hand gestures, and eye tracking. The goal of Samsung XR is to blend digital content
with the real world for immersive entertainment, productivity, and everyday computing—
without needing a phone or PC.

Key Features


Platform & AI

  • Runs Android XR, which blends digital content with your real environment and supports voice, hand, and eye interaction.

  • Google Gemini AI is integrated at the system level for contextual voice and gesture-based assistance.

Performance

  • Qualcomm Snapdragon XR2+ Gen 2 processor.

  • 16 GB RAM + 256 GB storage.

Display & Optics

  • Dual Micro-OLED displays with roughly 27 million pixels total (4K+ resolution).

  • Up to 90 Hz refresh and wide field of view (~109° × 100°).

Tacking & Sensors

  • HAnd, eys, head tracking, and voice control

  • Multiple cameras and sensors for precise trackng and passthrough AR

Audio & interaction

  • BUilt-in speaakers and a multi-mic array with spatial audio support

Comfort & Design

  • lightweight ergonomic frame and detachable light shield for immersion

  • External battery pack (adds comfort but also extra weight)
